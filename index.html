About Me

I am an Embedded Software Engineer and tech enthusiastwith over eight years of experience in developing safety-critical systems in the automotive industry. My expertise includes specially C development, model-based design with MATLAB/Simulink, functional safety (ISO 26262), and requirements/system engineering. I am currently expanding my skills in artificial intelligence through the IBM AI Professional Certificate on Coursera , with a strong interest in applying AI and optimization methods to real-world engineering challenges. I enjoy taking responsibility, working in cross-functional teams, and communicating openly and directly to solve complex problems with a structured approach.Outside of work, I am passionate about the outdoors – especially climbing, surfing, kitesurfing, and hiking. I also enjoy exploring technology, music production, and organizing small events.
